Podigee Podcast Player

A modern podcast player. For websites. From Podigee.

Why Podigee Podcast Player?

Your podcast deserves the best way to be played on the web

Embeddable

Embed the player into your own website, or allow you listeners to embed it on theirs.

Multi device

Works on all kinds of devices, including smartphones and of course desktop computers.

Episode info

Provides everything the listener needs to know about the currently playing episode in a single place.

Themeable

Adapt the player to your needs with our included themes or craft your own.

Extensible

Built with the future in mind, it's ready to keep up with the evolving podcast industry.

Chaptermarks

Gives your listeners more control over their experience and easy access to favorite parts.

Sharing

Allows your listeners to share links, audio snippets or embed the whole player.

Transcripts

Make your content accessible for a wider audience and better findable through search engines.

Playlist support

Your listeners can browse your complete back catalog in one player.

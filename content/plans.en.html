    Faq

    Frequently asked questions

    Q.

    How are "hours of audio encoding" calculated?

    When you upload a file we look at it's length in minutes. After the file gets encoded this amount of minutes is subtracted from your monthly quota.

    Q.

    What happens when I get over my quota?

    Don't worry: You can upload more than your quota allows. The system won't stop you. However, if you do it every month we will ask you to upgrade your plan eventually.

    Q.

    Can I configure Auphonic settings?

    Yes, you can influence which algorithms will be used and also configure them like you would on Auphonic.

    Q.

    Will my files expire?

    No. Everything you upload and publish stays online until you unpublish or delete it yourself. Promised!

    Q.

    Can I migrate an existing podcast?

    Of course! Actually we will do it for you. It is included with every paid plan. Just contact us and we'll set you up.

    Q.

    How many podcasts can I create? How does having multiple podcasts affect the quota?

    You can add as many podcasts as you like. The amount of minutes per month is shared between all of them.

    Q.

    Can I upload high-res (raw) audio files?

    Yes! Actually you should upload as high quality files as possible, quality enhancement and transcoding is already included in your plan.

    Q.

    Which payment methods do you accept?

    We accept all major credit cards. If you'd like to pay with a different method, like PayPal or wiretransfer, please contact us and we'll work something out!

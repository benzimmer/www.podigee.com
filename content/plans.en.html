    Faq

    Frequently asked questions

    Q.

    How are "hours of audio encoding" calculated?

    When you upload an audio file, we look at its length in minutes. Once the file gets encoded, this amount of minutes is subtracted from your monthly quota.

    Q.

    What happens when I get over my quota?

    If you occasionally hit your monthly encoding limit, you can easily buy additional one-time encoding hours, without having to upgrade directly to a more expensive plan.

    Q.

    Can I configure Auphonic settings?

    In some plans, you can edit the algorithms and also configure them like you would on Auphonic.

    Q.

    Will my files expire?

    No. Everything you upload and publish stays online until you unpublish or delete it yourself. Promised!

    Q.

    Can I migrate an existing podcast?

    Of course! Actually we will do it for you, for free, without using up your monthly quota. Just contact us and we'll set you up.

    Q.

    How many podcasts can I create? How does having multiple podcasts affect the quota?

    You can add as many podcasts as you like. The amount of minutes per month is shared across all of them.

    Q.

    Can I upload high-res (raw) audio files?

    Yes! Actually you should upload as high quality files as possible. Quality enhancement and transcoding are included in every plan.

    Q.

    Which payment methods do you accept?

    We accept all major credit cards, and if you pay in Euro, we accept payment with SEPA. If you'd like to pay with a different method, please contact us and we'll work it out!

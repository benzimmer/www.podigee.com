--- title: Facebook connection support keywords: - facebook - social-media - feature - publishing description: We added the possibility to post to facebook when a new episode is published date: '2014-02-06T00:00:00Z' ---

It is now possible to add a link to your Facebook page, next to other social media connections like Twitter or App.net. Go to your podcast editing page and choose the tab Connections. There, you will find a new field Facebook. Once you have filled out the Facebook field, the link to your page will be shown in your blog. If you use a custom theme, you can use the template attribute podcast.connections.facebook.

How does Podigee Premium actually work?

Podigee Premium allows you, as podcaster, to provide your individual, premium content to your supporters. For this you put freely configurable time subscriptions / packages and offer them to your audience. If a listener then chooses one of these packages, they will receive access to your premium listing for a paid period (via a secure, customized feed). If the time package is about to expire, we will send a reminder e-mail that will help your listeners easily renew their subscription. On the other hand, if the listener stops subscribing, his feed access ends automatically.

To showcase your offer accordingly, you can also create a landing page in a few minutes where you explain exactly what packages you offer and what content your fans may expect when they become supporters. This page is the one-stop shop for your premium listing - you can link to it from your website or promote it in your podcasts.

By the way: On your landing page, you can also offer packages and feeds for multiple of your podcast productions - you have all the freedom to decide.

Which payment methods are supported?

Your supporters can pay with credit cards or, in the EU, also with SEPA transfers.

Are gift or promotional codes possible?

Yes! Your listeners can also buy subscriptions for third parties - they will then receive a gift code, which they can simply send by email or print out. As a podcast producer, you can also create codes yourself and then distribute them as part of promo campaigns or sweepstakes.
